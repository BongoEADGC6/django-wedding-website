Masker's Barn

Getting There and Away

Masker's Barn is located in the Deserted Village section of Watchung Reservation.

For those coming from farther away, the venue is a 25 minute drive West from Newark International Airport.

For those of you driving, we will try to facilitate carpooling with other guests.

Things to Do

If you're here for a few days, here are some ideas of things to do.

Hiking

Watchung Reservation is FULL of hiking trails that you can spend hours in. Travel around and get lost in the scenery, especially during the time of year when the leaves are changing! Watchung Reservation Hiking Trail Guide

Escapology

Garwood's original real life PRIVATE Escape Game Experience (Never Play with Strangers!). Escapology is located in a prime spot next to Crunch Fitness in the Garwood Mall, up to 6 players will be challenged in this new attraction by working together to find clues, solve puzzles, and pick locks that will free them from the game room. Eat

Water & Wine provides elegant dining with excellent Italian food and a beautiful view of the Watchung Lake. Great meals can be found at The Rathskeller under the Dunellen Hotel, Texas Roadhouse , and The Scotchwood Diner.
