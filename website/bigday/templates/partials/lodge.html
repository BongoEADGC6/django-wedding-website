Schooley's Mountain has plenty of scenic hiking trails that you can spend hours in. Travel around and get lost in the scenery, especially during the time of year when the leaves are changing! Schooley's Mountain Hiking Trail Guide

Willowwood Arboretum

In 1908 two brothers used a small legacy to acquire their country home near Gladstone, New Jersey.
The two brothers, Henry and Robert Tubbs, and Henry’s adopted son, Dr. Benjamin Blackburn, developed this country place into the Willowwood Arboretum, New Jersey’s most comprehensive and longest continually operating Arboretum.ame room.
Willowwood Arboretum is open free to the public daily, 365 days yearly from 8am to dusk.
