Getting There and Away

Masker's Barn is located in the Deserted Village section of Watchung Reservation.

For those coming from farther away, the venue is a 25 minute drive West from Newark International Airport.

For those of you driving, we will try to facilitate carpooling with other guests.

Things to Do

If you're here for a few days, here are some ideas of things to do.

Hiking

Watchung Reservation is FULL of hiking trails that you can spend hours in. Travel around and get lost in the scenery, especially during the time of year when the leaves are changing! Watchung Reservation Hiking Trail Guide

Escapology

Garwood's original real life PRIVATE Escape Game Experience (Never Play with Strangers!). Escapology is located in a prime spot next to Crunch Fitness in the Garwood Mall, up to 6 players will be challenged in this new attraction by working together to find clues, solve puzzles, and pick locks that will free them from the game room. Website

Niagara Falls

A walk around the falls at sunset or at night can be gorgeous. You can also take a ferry through them, though prepare to get wet!

Around Niagara Falls is a thriving industry of haunted houses, a Butterfly Conservatory, Botanical Gardens, and even Niagara Falls Casino.

Golf

Plenty of scenic golf options around town, many of them using the same grounds for vines as for courses.

Eat

Great meals can be found at Garrison House, Treadwell, and Backhouse. Kitchen 72 inside the Two Sisters Vineyard serves delicious Italian food inside of an impressive estate.

Drink

Silversmith Brewing is an excellent local artisanal brewery offering offbeat new drinks.

Some informal later-night options include The Olde Angel Inn, Sandtrap, and the Irish Harp.
